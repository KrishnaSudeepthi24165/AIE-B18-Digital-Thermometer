Literature Review

Recent literature emphasizes the integration of Arduino with the LM35 sensor for digital thermometer design.

The LM35 is recognized as a reliable temperature sensor for various applications.

Studies highlight the importance of calibration to ensure measurement accuracy.

Many designs incorporate IoT connectivity for remote monitoring and data logging.

Key Insights:

  • Effective Signal Conditioning is essential to process the sensor output correctly.
  • Analog-to-Digital Conversion within Arduino facilitates precise data capture.
  • The system enables Real-Time Processing of temperature data.
  • The implementation results in a robust Digital Thermometer design.
  • Emphasis is placed on the role of Embedded Systems in such monitoring solutions.
