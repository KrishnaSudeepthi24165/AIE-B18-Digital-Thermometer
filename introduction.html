Introduction: Design and Implementation of a Digital Thermometer Using Arduino and LM35

Domain: Embedded systems and IoT-based temperature monitoring.

Purpose: Develop a real-time digital thermometer for accurate temperature measurement.

Key Components: Arduino microcontroller, LM35 temperature sensor, and an LCD display.

Working Principle: LM35 senses temperature, Arduino processes data, and the display shows readings.

Applications: Used in healthcare, weather monitoring, and industrial temperature control.

Advantages: Cost-effective, energy-efficient, and provides precise digital temperature readings.
